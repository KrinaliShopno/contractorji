Our Story

Every great idea starts small — and so did ContractorJi. What began as a simple effort to solve the daily challenges of finding trusted contractors in Surat soon turned into a powerful digital platform connecting buyers, homeowners, and businesses directly with verified contractors, labour teams, vendors, and suppliers across India.

We noticed that thousands of skilled contractors and material suppliers provide excellent work, but lack visibility and struggle to reach more customers. At the same time, buyers were unable to find genuine professionals without middlemen. That’s where ContractorJi stepped in — to build a trusted bridge between them.

The Beginning

Founded under Shopno Ecommerce Pvt Ltd, ContractorJi was created with a simple mission — to make hiring contractors easy, transparent, and reliable. What started with local vendors and contractors in Gujarat quickly expanded into multiple categories covering construction materials, flooring, electrical services, plumbing, fabrication, marble work, inlay design, interior work, and more.

Our Vision Behind ContractorJi

We believe in the power of skilled Indian workers and the spirit of local businesses. ContractorJi isn’t just a directory platform — it's a digital movement that empowers the entire construction industry of India.

Our vision is to make sure every contractor, vendor, or worker — no matter their size — gets equal opportunity, genuine customers, and the visibility they deserve.

What Makes Us Different
  • We connect buyers directly with verified contractors — no middlemen.
  • We focus on genuine listings and real business opportunities.
  • We give digital presence to small and medium contractors who rely on trust and quality.
  • We operate with transparency, honesty, and Indian values.
The Road Ahead

Our goal is to make ContractorJi a trusted name for every Indian household and business. We are continuously improving, growing, and adding new categories. Together, we aim to make India’s contractor–community stronger, smarter, and better connected than ever.

"When the right contractor gets the right work, India grows."
