User & Contracterji Policy

This User & Contracterji Policy (“Policy”) explains the rights, responsibilities, and conduct expected from users and listed contractors/business owners (“Contracterji”) on www.contractorji.com, operated by SHOPNO ECOMMERCE PVT LTD (“we”, “our”, “us”). By accessing or using our platform, you agree to follow this Policy as per Indian law.

1. Role of ContractorJi

ContractorJi is an online listing and business networking platform that enables contractors, service providers, and vendors (“Contracterji”) to list their business details so users (“Buyers”) can contact them directly. We do NOT sell, supply, or manage any service listed on the platform.

2. Definitions
  • “Contracterji” – any contractor, service provider, or business listed on our platform.
  • “User” – any person using the platform to find and contact Contracterji.
3. Responsibilities of Contracterji (Contractors / Service Providers)

All Contracterji using ContractorJi must:

  • Provide true, complete, and verified business information.
  • Ensure uploaded images, pricing, and details are accurate and not misleading.
  • Comply with applicable Indian business laws, GST, and trade rules.
  • Not post illegal, inappropriate, or harmful content.
  • Take responsibility for discussions, quotations, and work execution with users.
  • Handle inquiries, complaints, and payments directly with the user.

Any false or misleading information may lead to suspension or permanent removal of the Contracterji account without notice.

4. Responsibilities of Users (Buyers / Visitors)

Users must:

  • Use the platform only for genuine business or service inquiries.
  • Verify contractor details independently before hiring or making payment.
  • Not misuse contact details or send spam, abusive, or fraudulent messages.
  • Respect the privacy and data of listed Contracterji.
5. Platform Limitations

ContractorJi does NOT:

  • Guarantee service quality, delivery, or availability.
  • Participate in payments or contracts between users and Contracterji.
  • Provide warranty, replacement, or refund for contractor services.

All interactions happen directly between User and Contracterji at their own risk.

6. Paid Listings & Membership

Contracterji who subscribe to paid listings will receive enhanced visibility according to their chosen plan. Activation of a paid plan does NOT guarantee sales, orders, or user responses. Refunds & membership policies are governed by our Refund & Cancellation Policy.

7. Misuse & Account Termination

We reserve the right to suspend or remove any User or Contracterji account that:

  • Provides fake or misleading information,
  • Engages in spam, fraud, harassment, or abusive behavior,
  • Violates Indian laws or ContractorJi platform rules.
8. Data Privacy & Communication

Users and Contracterji agree that shared information (contact numbers, emails, business info) may be used for communication related to genuine business inquiries. For data protection details, see our Privacy Policy.

9. Legal Jurisdiction

This Policy is governed by Indian law. Any dispute arising from this Policy shall fall under the exclusive jurisdiction of courts in Surat, Gujarat, India.
