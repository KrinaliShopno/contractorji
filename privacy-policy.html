Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 08 November 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how, operating under the brand name ContractorJi, collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you use our website www.contractorji.com. This Policy is compliant with the Information Technology Act, 2000, the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011, and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) of India.

1. About ContractorJi

ContractorJi is an online local listing and B2B + B2C service discovery platform that helps contractors, material suppliers, workers, and service providers showcase their business and connect directly with buyers. We do not take part in transactions, payments, or services between buyers and contractors.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following details during registration or profile creation:

  • Business name, contact person name, and designation
  • Business address, state, city, and pin code
  • Email address and phone number
  • Service categories and business details
  • Website link or social media profiles (if provided)

We may also collect technical information like browser type, IP address, device type, and time spent on pages to improve user experience.

3. Purpose Of Collecting Information

We collect and use your information for the following purposes:

  • To display contractor or vendor listings on the ContractorJi platform
  • To connect buyers directly with verified service providers
  • To communicate regarding inquiries, support, or promotional updates

All collected data is stored on secure servers within India. We follow industry-standard security protocols to protect your data. However, no electronic system is fully secure, and users are advised to share information at their own discretion.

5. Sharing Of Information

We do not sell or trade your personal information. Data may be shared only in the following cases:

  • With verified buyers who wish to contact listed contractors
  • With third-party service providers (example: analytics, hosting) for operational use
  • When required by law, court order, or government authority
6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to improve website performance and user experience. You may disable cookies from your browser settings, but certain functions may not work properly.

7. Your Rights Under DPDP Act, 2023

As per Indian data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access and correct your data
  • Withdraw consent for data usage (where applicable)
  • Request deletion of your personal information

To exercise these rights, please email us at: contact@contractorji.com

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the content or privacy policies of those websites.

9. Data Retention

We retain user data only for as long as necessary to provide services or as required by Indian law.

10. Children’s Privacy

ContractorJi is intended for users above 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

11. Limitation Of Liability

ContractorJi operates only as a listing and discovery platform. We are not liable for any disputes, payments, losses, or damages arising between buyers and contractors.

12. Updates To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to comply with legal standards or reflect changes in our platform. Updated policies will be posted with a revised effective date.
